Privacy Policy for Pet Weight Tracker

Last Updated: January 29, 2025

Summary

Pet Weight Tracker does not collect any data. All information stays on your device.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any information. Pet Weight Tracker does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information or data about you or your pets on external servers.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes pet names, birthdays, species, weight entries, and notes. This data never leaves your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share any data because we do not have access to any data.

Third-Party Services

Pet Weight Tracker does not use any third-party services, analytics, or advertising.
